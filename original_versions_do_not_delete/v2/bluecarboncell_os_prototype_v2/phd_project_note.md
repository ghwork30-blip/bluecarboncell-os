
# How to use this prototype in the PhD proposal

In the research proposal, you can describe this prototype as:

> An early-stage digital decision-support prototype will be developed to evaluate MCFC-based ship retrofit feasibility. The prototype, named BlueCarbonCell OS, will estimate CO2 capture potential, heat recovery, electricity generation, pressure-loss risk, thermal suitability, uncertainty ranges and pilot-readiness indicators for different vessel profiles.

Suggested phrase:

BlueCarbonCell OS will not be presented as a certified design tool during the PhD. Instead, it will be used as a transparent research prototype for scenario comparison, uncertainty analysis, system-boundary testing and translation of MCFC cell/stack assumptions into ship-level retrofit indicators.

Good figures to export from the app:

1. CO2 captured vs remaining.
2. Score breakdown.
3. Dynamic exhaust profile.
4. Monte Carlo feasibility distribution.
5. Scenario comparison chart.
